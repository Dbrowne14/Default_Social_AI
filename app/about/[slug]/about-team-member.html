Profile

The person who makes sure it actually ships.

David joined Default Social to build the engineering function from the ground up. A decade of production experience across commerce, editorial and real-time systems means he treats performance, accessibility and maintainability as first-class deliverables — not things bolted on before launch.

He works closest with strategy and creative, translating an ambition into an architecture the team can move fast inside. Most of what he ships is invisible: the render pipeline, the content model, the deploy story — the parts that decide whether a beautiful idea holds up under real traffic.

Outside of client work he maintains the studio's internal component library and mentors the junior developers through their first production launches.
